## ğŸš€ Quick Start

### Step 1: Upload Your File
Choose one of two methods:

**Method A: File Upload**
1. Click the "Upload Audio/Video" tab
2. Drag & drop your file OR click to browse
3. Select an audio or video file (up to 100MB)

**Method B: URL Input**
1. Click the "Enter URL" tab
2. Paste the URL of your audio/video file
3. Click outside the input to confirm

### Step 2: Configure (Optional)
- Toggle "Enable Speaker Identification" if your audio has multiple speakers
- This helps identify who said what

### Step 3: Transcribe
1. Click the "Transcribe Audio" button
2. Wait for processing (usually 1-3 minutes)
3. View your results!

### For Best Results
1. **Audio Quality Matters**
   - Use clear, high-quality recordings
   - Minimize background noise
   - Ensure good microphone placement

   - Enable for interviews, meetings, podcasts

## ğŸ“Š What to Expect

### Processing Time
- **Short files** (< 5 min): ~30-60 seconds
- **Medium files** (5-15 min): ~1-2 minutes
- **Long files** (15-30 min): ~2-4 minutes
- **Very long files** (30+ min): ~4-10 minutes

### Accuracy
- **Clear audio**: 90-95% accuracy
- **Good audio**: 85-90% accuracy
- **Fair audio**: 75-85% accuracy
- **Poor audio**: 60-75% accuracy

### Speaker Identification
- **2-3 speakers**: Excellent accuracy
- **4-6 speakers**: Good accuracy
- **7+ speakers**: Fair accuracy
- **Similar voices**: May have confusion
